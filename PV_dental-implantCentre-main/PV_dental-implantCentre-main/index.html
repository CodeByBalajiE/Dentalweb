About Us

We Care For Your Dental Health

Welcome to PV Dental & Implant Centre, where we believe in creating healthy, confident smiles with expert care and advanced technology.
Led by Dr. S. Dhanalakshmi, a skilled Dental Surgeon (Reg. No: 13643), our clinic is dedicated to providing top-quality dental care in a comfortable and friendly environment. We specialize in a wide range of dental services, including general dentistry, dental implants, orthodontics, and preventive care. Our team is committed to using state-of-the-art equipment and the latest techniques to ensure pain-free and effective treatments.
At PV Dental & Implant Centre, patient satisfaction and oral health are our top priorities. We strive to make every visit a pleasant experience, ensuring that you leave with a healthier and brighter smile!
